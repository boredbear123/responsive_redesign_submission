RESPONSIVE REDESIGN

CALIENTE
MEXICAN GRILL

I chose Calientes Mexican Grill because it is one of my favorite places to eat out on Thayer street, and it is also a custom-made restaurant site.

Calientes Current Website

PROBLEMS

The home screen of the website has a video background with the navigation bar and logo overlaid. When the video is lighter it is hard to see the white text of the navigation bar, and the video’s height makes it so that it take sup the entire screen and doesn't make it clear that you can scroll down on the page

When you scroll down on the homepage, the order and menu are not the first things to come up, so in terms of efficiency it is not that efficient to get people to the menu or to order online. (It does have it in the navigation bar, but users especially first time users tend to want to scroll on the page to get their bearings

The homepage is also cluttered and does not use white space/ blank space well. They have some awesome pictures and videos but they need to be leveraged the right way

In the reviews section on the homepage there are two buttons to aid in the horizontal scroll of the featured reviews but the buttons are not obvious what they do, even upon clicking on them.

When using the WebAim WAVE to evaluate the accessibility of the website, they actually did a relatively good job in terms of having alt-tags and structural elements, but there were 11 Contrast Errors, with “Very low Contrast”, most of them were related to the navigation bar and the background video, which I had pointed out above.
